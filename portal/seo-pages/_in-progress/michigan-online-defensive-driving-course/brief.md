## Key Michigan Context (critical for E-E-A-T)

- **Approving body:** Michigan Department of State (also called Michigan Secretary of State / SOS) — NOT "DMV"
- **Official program name:** Basic Driver Improvement Course (BDIC)
- **Course length:** 4 hours minimum (set by Michigan law)
- **Eligibility trigger:** State mails an eligibility letter; driver has 60 days to enroll and complete
- **Point benefit:** Michigan SOS will NOT add points from the ticket to driving record
- **Insurance benefit:** Insurer discretion; some offer up to 10% discount for 3 years
- **Lifetime limit:** BDIC point avoidance can only be used ONCE per lifetime
- **Eligibility requirement:** 2 or fewer points on record, minor non-criminal violation, must be on eligible infraction list
- **Completion notification:** Course sponsor notifies Michigan Department of State electronically
