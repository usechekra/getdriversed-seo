# Keyword Formulas & Placement Rules

Everything about where keywords go and how to write them.

## Title Tag Formula

```
[State] [Duration or Course Qualifier] [Course Type Keyword] | Get Drivers Ed
```

**Char budget:** 50‚Äì60. Go shorter if possible, never longer.

**Modifier bank (use ONE only):**
- Duration: `5-Hour`, `6-Hour`, `8-Hour`, `24-Hour`, `30-Hour`
- Audience: `Teen`, `Adult`, `Senior`, `Beginner`
- Format: `Online`, `Virtual`
- Authority: `DMV-Approved`, `BMV-Approved`, `State-Approved` (only if space allows AND primary KW doesn't already imply approval)

**Separator:** Pipe `|` only. Never `‚Äì`, `-`, `‚Äî`, `/`, `:`.

**State/course tokens:**
- Full state name, not abbreviated (`New York` not `NY`)
- Exact course terminology the agency uses (`Pre-Licensing` for NY, `Drivers Ed` for most, `Driver Improvement` for FL, `TIPIC Course` for OH teens)

**Three variants every optimization delivers:**

- **V1 ‚Äî Primary-match (safe default):**
  `[State] [Duration] [Course Type] | Get Drivers Ed`
  Example: `New York 5-Hour Online Pre-Licensing Course | Get Drivers Ed`

- **V2 ‚Äî Benefit-led:**
  `Online [State] [Course] ‚Äî [Key Benefit] | Get Drivers Ed`
  Example: `Online Ohio Drivers Ed ‚Äî State-Approved | Get Drivers Ed`

- **V3 ‚Äî Authority-led:**
  `[State] DMV-Approved [Course Type] Online | Get Drivers Ed`
  Example: `California DMV-Approved Drivers Ed Online | Get Drivers Ed`

Ship V1 by default. Test V2/V3 where volume justifies.

**Anti-patterns:**
- ‚ùå Dates in titles (`2026`, `Updated 2026`)
- ‚ùå Emojis, symbols, ALL CAPS
- ‚ùå Duplicate brand token
- ‚ùå Stop-word stuffing (`The Best #1 Top Rated`)

## Meta Description Formula

```
Get Drivers Ed offers [primary KW naturally phrased], designed for [benefit 1] 
and [benefit 2] to meet [authority anchor]. [CTA].
```

**Char budget:** 140‚Äì155.

**CTA bank:**
- `Enroll now!`
- `Get started today!`
- `Sign up today!`
- `Start learning today!`
- `Register online now!`

**Benefit bank (choose 2):**
- `convenient`, `self-paced`, `flexible learning`, `mobile-friendly`, `affordable`
- `100% online`, `instant certificate`, `DMV-ready`, `state-authorized`
- `beginner-friendly`, `interactive lessons`, `expert-guided`

**Authority anchor bank:**
- `DMV requirements` / `BMV requirements` / `DOL requirements` / `DPS requirements` (match the state)
- `state licensing standards`
- `[State] driver's license requirements`

**Three variants:**

- **V1 ‚Äî NP|accel pattern:**
  `Get Drivers Ed offers a New York 5-Hour online pre-licensing course, designed for convenient, self-paced learning to meet DMV requirements. Enroll now!`

- **V2 ‚Äî Benefit-led:**
  `Earn your NYMV-278 certificate fast. Our New York 5-Hour online pre-licensing course is self-paced, mobile-friendly, and DMV-approved. Sign up today!`

- **V3 ‚Äî Question-led:**
  `Need a New York 5-hour pre-licensing course online? Get Drivers Ed offers a flexible, DMV-approved program with instant certificate. Enroll now!`

**Anti-patterns:**
- ‚ùå Primary KW buried after word 15
- ‚ùå Two CTAs (pick one)
- ‚ùå 5 benefits listed (pick 2)
- ‚ùå Mirroring title verbatim

## URL Slug Rules

**Pattern:** `https://getdriversed.com/courses-details/[state-kebab]-[course-kebab]/en`

**Rules:**
1. Kebab-case
2. Full state name hyphenated (`new-york`, not `ny`)
3. Under 60 chars slug portion
4. No stop words (`the`, `of`, `for`, `a`, `an`)
5. `/en` suffix if multi-lang (current convention)
6. No trailing slash after `/en` (current convention)
7. Consistent namespace ‚Äî use `/courses-details/`, not `/course/`

**When changing a slug:** ALWAYS 301 old ‚Üí new. Never change without a redirect.

- **Primary KW:** 1.2% ‚Äì 1.8%. For a 1,400-word page: 17‚Äì25 occurrences.
